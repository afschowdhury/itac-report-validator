AR No.7 – Purchase Optimum Sized Air Compressor with More Suitable Substitutes

Purchasing an optimally sized air compressor with more suitable substitutes will enhance energy efficiency in compressed air systems. This equipment upgrade will yield $3,709 in total cost savings and incur an implementation cost of $16,949. The project has a payback period of 4.57 years and will reduce CO₂ emissions by 14 tons annually, with annual energy savings of 36,365 kWh.
