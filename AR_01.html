AR No. 1 – Utilize Higher Efficiency Lamps and/or Ballasts

Utilizing higher-efficiency lamps and/or ballasts will enhance lighting system performance while reducing energy consumption. This energy efficiency measure will yield $771 in total cost savings and incur an implementation cost of $675. The project has a payback period of 0.88 years and will reduce CO₂ emissions by 3 tons annually, with annual energy savings of 6,806 kWh.
