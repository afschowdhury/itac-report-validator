AR No. 2 – Install Sub-metering Equipment

Installing sub-metering equipment will enable better monitoring and management of electrical energy consumption. This energy efficiency measure will yield $3,313 in total cost savings and incur an implementation cost of $3,000. The project has a payback period of 0.91 years and will reduce CO₂ emissions by 12 tons annually, with annual energy savings of 32,484 kWh.
