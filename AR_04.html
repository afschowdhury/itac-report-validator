AR No. 4 – Replace Existing HVAC with Higher Efficiency Model

Replacing the existing HVAC system with a higher efficiency model will optimize energy consumption and improve overall system performance. This energy efficiency measure will yield $1,488 in total cost savings and incur an implementation cost of $5,190. The project has a payback period of 3.49 years and will reduce CO₂ emissions by 6 tons annually, with annual energy savings of 14,586 kWh.
